There's plenty of AI benchmarking out there — reasoning scores, coding tests, math olympiads. What's rarer is watching AI models interact with each other in messy, open-ended scenarios where the rules are social, not numerical.

That's what this site does. I build a Python engine that acts as game master, then pit ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and others against each other in scenarios ranging from Monopoly to simulated diplomatic crises between fictional nations.

The experiments are real. The write-ups are honest. And the results are often genuinely surprising.
