How scoring works

Not every experiment has a winner — a legislative simulation might end in deadlock, which is itself a result worth writing about. Where a clear outcome exists (one player bankrupt in Monopoly, one nation surrendering in a military scenario), that's logged here.

Win rates are tracked per model family, not per specific model version. When a new model version is tested against an older scenario, results are noted separately and both are logged. The scoreboard is a rough guide, not a rigorous benchmark.

Prompts, parameters, and methodology for each experiment are published in the Parameters section of each article. Reproducibility is a goal, not a guarantee — LLM outputs are non-deterministic.
